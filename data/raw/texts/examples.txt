# Hirnu Language Examples

## Basic Declarative Sentences

EN: The man walks in the dark.
HI: Grim halin hålin.

EN: The child looks at the stars.
HI: Barn lugnir starnir.

EN: The sky is shining now.
HI: Himrin skirr nu.

EN: The men walk in the dark.
HI: Grimir halin hålin.

EN: The children look at the stars.
HI: Barnir lugnir starnir.

## Pronouns in Use

EN: I walk in the dark.
HI: Ek halin hålin.

EN: You will look at the stars.
HI: Du skal lugnin starnir.

EN: He shines now.
HI: Han skirr nu.

EN: We walk in the dark.
HI: Ekir halin hålin.

EN: You all look at the stars.
HI: Duir lugnir starnir.

EN: They shine now.
HI: Hanir skirr nu.

## Future Tense (with "skal")

EN: A man will walk in the dark.
HI: Grim skal halin hålin.

EN: The child will look at the stars.
HI: Barn skal lugnin starnir.

EN: The sky will shine.
HI: Himrin skal skirr.

## Interrogative Sentences

EN: Where is the man walking?
HI: Var grim halin?

EN: Will the darkness disappear?
HI: Ef hålin häbr?

EN: Are you looking at the stars in the sky?
HI: Du lugnir starnir var himrin?

EN: Where are the children?
HI: Var barnir?

## Conditional Sentences (with "ef")

EN: If a man comes walking in the darkness, the stars will shine.
HI: Ef grim skal halin hålin, starnir skirr.

EN: If a child looks at the sky, the darkness will disappear.
HI: Ef barn lugnir himrin, hålin häbr.

EN: If a man were to walk in the darkness, the stars would shine.
HI: Ef grim halskan hålin, starnir skal skirr.

## Imperative Sentences

EN: Walk in the darkness!
HI: Halin hålin!

EN: Look at the stars!
HI: Lugnin starnir!

EN: Shine in the sky!
HI: Skirin himrin!

## Particles in Use

EN: The men walk in the dark.
HI: Grimir halin var hålin.

EN: The child looks at the stars now.
HI: Barn lugnir nu starnir.

EN: If the darkness disappears by the stars, the man will walk.
HI: Ef hålin häbr starnir, grim skal halin.

## Poetic Sentences (Flexible Word Order)

EN: Now the darkness disappears in the sky, the child looks at the brilliance of the stars.
HI: Nu hålin häbr var himrin, barn lugnir starnir skirr.

EN: If a man comes to walk in the darkness, the sky now shines for the child.
HI: Ef grim skal halin hålin, himrin skirr nu var barn.

EN: The sky shines in the darkness, the man looks at the child by the stars.
HI: Himrin skirr hålin, grim lugnir barn var starnir.

EN: Now the darkness disappears in the sky, the stars shine in the child's eyes.
HI: Nu hålin häbr var himrin, starnir skirr barn lugnir.

## Complex Sentences

EN: The men will walk where the stars shine.
HI: Grimir skal halin var starnir skirr.

EN: If the child looks now, the man will walk.
HI: Ef barn lugnir nu, grim skal halin.

EN: The darkness disappears when the sky shines.
HI: Hålin häbr ef himrin skirr.
