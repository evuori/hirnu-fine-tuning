# Barn var Starnir
# The Child and the Stars

HI: Barn lugnir himrin var vono. Starnir skirr hålin. Barn skal lugnin starnir.
EN: A child looks at the sky in the night. The stars shine in the darkness. The child will look at the stars.

HI: "Var starnir?" barn lugnir.
EN: "Where are the stars?" the child looks.

HI: Grim halin var barn. "Starnir skirr var himrin," grim lugnir barn.
EN: A man walks to the child. "The stars shine in the sky," the man tells the child.

HI: "Du skal halin habr ek?" barn lugnir grim.
EN: "You will walk with me?" the child asks the man.

HI: "Ek skal halin habr du," grim skirr.
EN: "I will walk with you," the man agrees.

HI: Grimir halin var hålin. Barn lugnir starnir skirr. Himrin skirr nu.
EN: The men walk in the darkness. The child looks at the shining stars. The sky shines now.

HI: Ef barn lugnir starnir, barn skirr habr starnir.
EN: If the child looks at the stars, the child shines with the stars.

HI: Nu hålin häbr var himrin. Starnir skirr barn lugnir. Barn skal halin var starnir.
EN: Now the darkness disappears in the sky. The stars shine for the child looking. The child will walk toward the stars.

HI: "Starnir skal skirr var ek?" barn lugnir grim.
EN: "Will the stars shine for me?" the child asks the man.

HI: "Starnir skal skirr var du, ef du lugnir himrin," grim lugnir barn.
EN: "The stars will shine for you, if you look at the sky," the man tells the child.

HI: Barn lugnir himrin nu. Starnir skirr. Barn skirr habr starnir. Himrin skirr barn.
EN: The child looks at the sky now. The stars shine. The child shines with the stars. The sky shines for the child.

HI: Vono häbr. Gunava skirr. Barn skal halin var grim. Starnir skal skirr var vono.
EN: The night disappears. Morning shines. The child will walk with the man. The stars will shine in the night.

---

## Vocabulary Used in Story

- barn = child
- barnir = children
- grim = man
- grimir = men
- starn = star
- starnir = stars
- himrin = sky
- hålin = darkness
- vono = night
- gunava = morning
- lugnir = looks/look
- lugnin = to look
- halin = walks/walk (also "to walk")
- skirr = shines/shine
- var = where/in/at
- nu = now
- ef = if
- habr = with/by
- skal = will
- häbr = disappears
- ek = I
- du = you
- han = he/she

## Grammar Features Demonstrated

1. SVO word order: "Barn lugnir starnir"
2. Plural with -ir: starn ’ starnir, grim ’ grimir
3. Future with skal: "Barn skal halin"
4. Particles: var (where/in), nu (now), ef (if), habr (with)
5. Questions with particles: "Var starnir?"
6. Conditional sentences: "Ef barn lugnir starnir..."
7. Pronouns: ek (I), du (you)
8. Poetic word order: "Nu hålin häbr var himrin"
